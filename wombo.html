What is Dream by WOMBO?

Dream by WOMBO is an AI-powered art generator that transforms your text prompts into stunning digital artwork in seconds. Simply describe your vision, choose an art style, and let Dream’s AI create beautiful images—perfect for inspiration, social media, or creative projects.

Key Features

  • Create unique artwork from text prompts
  • Wide variety of artistic styles and filters
  • Easy to use on web and mobile apps
  • Download, share, or print your creations
  • Community gallery for inspiration

AI Technology

Dream by WOMBO uses advanced AI image generation models to interpret your prompts and produce original, high-resolution artwork in many styles.

Pricing

Free to use with basic features and watermarked images. Paid plans unlock premium styles, higher-resolution downloads, and commercial use rights. See Dream by WOMBO’s website for details.

Use Cases
