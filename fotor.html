What is Fotor AI?

Fotor AI is an AI-powered photo editor and image generator that makes it easy to enhance, retouch, and transform images. It offers tools for background removal, AI image generation, enhancement, photo effects, and more—all online and with a user-friendly interface.

Key Features

  • AI-powered photo enhancement and retouching
  • Text-to-image AI art generator
  • Background remover and object remover
  • Photo effects, filters, and collages
  • Batch processing and templates

AI Technology

Fotor leverages deep learning for image enhancement, object detection, and generative art, providing fast and high-quality results for both beginners and professionals.

Pricing

Free plan available with core tools. Premium plans unlock advanced features and higher-resolution exports. See Fotor’s website for current pricing.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Easy to use, web-based and fast
  • Wide variety of AI photo tools and generators
  • Good for both casual and professional use

Cons:

  • Best features require a subscription
  • Free plan has some limits on export size and access

Use Cases

Perfect for social media creators, marketers, photographers, and anyone needing quick, creative image editing or AI art generation.
