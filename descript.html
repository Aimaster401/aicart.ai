What is Descript?

Descript is a revolutionary AI-powered audio and video editing platform. It offers features like transcription, overdub, screen recording, and multi-track editing, making content creation simple and collaborative.

Key Features

  • Automatic transcription
  • Overdub (AI voice cloning)
  • Text-based audio/video editing
  • Screen and webcam recording
  • Multi-user collaboration
  • Podcast and video publishing tools

AI Technology

Descript leverages state-of-the-art speech recognition and AI voice synthesis to enable fast, accurate editing and content creation.
