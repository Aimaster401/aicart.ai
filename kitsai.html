What is Kits AI?

Kits AI is an advanced AI platform for music creators, offering voice cloning, AI music production, and tools for remixing and transforming audio. It's great for musicians, producers, and content creators who want to experiment with new sounds and voices.

Key Features

  • AI voice cloning and transformation
  • Text-to-singing and text-to-speech
  • Music remixing and stem separation
  • Library of AI voices and instruments
  • Integration with DAWs and music software

AI Technology

Kits AI uses neural network models for accurate voice cloning, vocal transformation, and music stem separation, enabling innovative audio production workflows.
