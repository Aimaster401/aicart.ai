What is Papercup?

Papercup is an AI-powered dubbing and voice translation platform that enables users to localize videos into multiple languages using synthetic voices. It allows for fast, accurate, and scalable video localization for creators, media companies, and enterprises without the need for traditional human dubbing.

Key Features

  • AI dubbing with synthetic, natural-sounding voices
  • Automatic speech translation into multiple languages
  • Human-quality review and editing options
  • Scalable for large video libraries
  • Supports video and audio formats

AI Technology

Papercup uses advanced speech recognition, neural machine translation, and AI voice synthesis to deliver high-quality dubbed videos with fast turnaround times.

Pricing

Pricing is based on minutes of video processed and service level. Custom quotes for enterprises. See Papercup’s website for details.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Fast and scalable video localization
  • High-quality synthetic voices
  • Reduces cost and time compared to human dubbing

Cons:

  • Most accurate results with human review (optional)
  • Best suited for enterprise and large projects

Use Cases

Ideal for YouTubers, media companies, e-learning platforms, and enterprises looking to reach multilingual audiences with localized video content.
