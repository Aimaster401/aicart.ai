What is Writer.com?

Writer.com is an enterprise-grade AI writing platform designed to help teams create clear, consistent, and on-brand content at scale. It offers real-time suggestions for writing, grammar, style, and brand voice, ensuring every piece of content matches company guidelines.

Key Features

  • Brand voice and style guide enforcement
  • Grammar, spelling, and clarity suggestions
  • Custom terminology management
  • Content scoring and analytics
  • Integrations with Slack, Chrome, Word, Figma, and more

AI Technology

Writer.com uses AI and NLP to provide personalized content recommendations, ensuring brand alignment and high writing standards across teams.
