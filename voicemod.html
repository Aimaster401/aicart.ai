What is Voicemod?

Voicemod is a real-time AI voice changer and soundboard for gamers, streamers, and content creators. Change your voice live, add sound effects, and create unique audio experiences for Discord, Twitch, Zoom, and more.

Key Features

  • Real-time AI voice changing
  • Extensive library of voice filters and effects
  • Custom soundboard for live audio triggers
  • Voice lab for creating new voices
  • Integration with popular games and streaming platforms

AI Technology

Voicemod uses deep learning and digital signal processing to modify voice characteristics, add effects, and generate new, realistic or fantastical voices in real time.

Pricing

Free version available with core features. Paid Pro version unlocks premium voices, effects, and advanced soundboard capabilities. See Voicemod’s website for details.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Fun and creative for gaming and streaming
  • Easy to use in real time
  • Voice lab for custom voices

Cons:

  • Some effects are Pro-only
  • Windows focused (limited Mac support)

Use Cases

Great for gamers, streamers, roleplayers, and anyone who wants to add unique audio effects to online interactions or video content.
