What is Veed.io?

Veed.io is an AI-powered online video editor and creation platform for marketers, businesses, and content creators. It offers easy-to-use tools for editing, subtitling, transcribing, and enhancing videos directly in your browser.

Key Features

  • AI video editing tools (cut, trim, crop, resize)
  • Automatic subtitles and transcription
  • AI text-to-video and video-to-text
  • Templates for social media and marketing
  • Stock videos, images, and music

AI Technology

Veed.io uses AI for automatic subtitle generation, video translation, background noise removal, and content creation from text prompts.

Pricing

Free plan with watermarks and basic features. Paid plans offer higher resolution, watermark removal, and premium AI features. See Veed.io’s website for current pricing.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Very user-friendly, no software download needed
  • Powerful AI tools for subtitles and editing
  • Great for social media and marketing content

Cons:

  • Free plan has watermarks
  • Best features require a subscription

Use Cases

Ideal for marketers, content creators, businesses, and educators who need fast, professional video editing and AI automation.
