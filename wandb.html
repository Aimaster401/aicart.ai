What is Weights & Biases?

Weights & Biases (W&B) is a platform for machine learning experiment tracking, model management, and collaboration. It helps teams visualize metrics, compare runs, version datasets, and manage the full lifecycle of ML projects.

Key Features

  • Experiment tracking and visualization
  • Dataset and model versioning
  • Hyperparameter management
  • Team collaboration and reporting
  • Integration with popular ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras, etc.)

AI Technology

W&B provides interactive dashboards powered by robust APIs, allowing seamless tracking and sharing of machine learning progress.

Pricing

Free for individuals and academic use. Paid team and enterprise plans available — see the W&B website for details.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Easy integration with ML frameworks
  • Rich visualizations and collaboration tools
  • Supports reproducible research

Cons:

  • Some features require payment
  • Cloud-based; data privacy considerations

Use Cases

Perfect for ML practitioners, researchers, and teams who need to track, compare, and share machine learning experiments.
