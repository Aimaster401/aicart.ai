What is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that provides direct answers to your questions with citations. Unlike traditional search engines, Perplexity AI summarizes information from multiple sources and provides a concise and sourced answer, saving you time and effort.

Key Features

  • AI-powered search and answer generation
  • Citations for all information provided
  • Conversational search interface
  • Focus on factual accuracy and reliability

AI Technology

Perplexity AI utilizes large language models (LLMs) to understand your queries and retrieve relevant information from the web. It then synthesizes the information into a coherent answer, citing the original sources to ensure transparency and trustworthiness.
