What is InVideo?

InVideo is an AI-powered online video creation platform that allows users to create stunning videos in minutes. With its vast template library, AI text-to-video, stock assets, and smart editing tools, it's perfect for marketers, businesses, and content creators.

Key Features

  • AI text-to-video generation
  • Extensive video templates and stock library
  • Automated voiceovers and subtitles
  • One-click resizing for social platforms
  • Branding and customization options

AI Technology

InVideo uses AI for script analysis, automatic scene selection, voiceover generation, and smart editing recommendations.

Pricing

Free plan with watermark and basic features. Paid plans unlock watermark removal, premium templates, and advanced capabilities. See InVideo’s website for current pricing.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Fast and easy video creation
  • Rich template and asset library
  • AI automation for editing and voiceovers

Cons:

  • Free plan includes watermark
  • Best features are subscription-based

Use Cases

Ideal for marketers, social media managers, educators, and anyone wanting to quickly create professional-quality videos.
