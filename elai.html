What is Elai?

Elai is an AI-powered platform that enables users to generate videos with lifelike AI avatars simply from text. It's perfect for businesses, educators, and content creators who want to produce professional videos without cameras or actors.

Key Features

  • AI avatars for video presentations
  • Text-to-video generation
  • Multi-language voiceovers
  • Custom avatar creation
  • Branding and template options

AI Technology

Elai uses advanced AI for lifelike avatar animation, natural-sounding voice generation, and automatic video assembly from text input.

Pricing

Free trial with limited credits. Paid plans unlock more minutes, custom avatars, and premium features. See Elai’s website for current pricing.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • No filming or actors required
  • Supports over 65 languages
  • Highly realistic AI avatars

Cons:

  • Free trial is limited
  • Best features require a subscription

Use Cases

Great for training, marketing, explainer videos, and global communication without the need for a camera crew.
