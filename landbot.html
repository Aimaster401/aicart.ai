What is Landbot AI?

Landbot AI is a no-code chatbot builder designed to create highly interactive conversations and automate customer interactions. It provides businesses with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface to design custom chat flows that enhance engagement and boost conversions.

Key Features

  • No-code chatbot builder with visual workflow creation
  • Customizable conversational automation
  • Multi-channel deployment (website, WhatsApp, Messenger, etc.)
  • Lead generation and customer support optimization
  • Integrations with CRM, analytics, and other business tools

AI Technology

Landbot AI uses rule-based and AI-driven automation to guide users through dynamic chatbot conversations, improving customer engagement and streamlining interactions.

Pricing

Free plan available for basic chatbot creation. Paid subscriptions offer advanced automation, integrations, and premium chatbot capabilities. Visit Landbot’s website for detailed pricing.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • User-friendly drag-and-drop chatbot builder
  • Great for lead generation and interactive engagement
  • Multi-platform support for better accessibility

Cons:

  • Advanced automation features require a subscription
  • Some complex workflows may require extra customization

Use Cases

Ideal for businesses looking to create engaging chatbot experiences, automate customer support, and optimize lead conversion with AI-driven automation.
