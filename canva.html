What is Canva AI?

Canva AI refers to the collection of artificial intelligence-powered tools integrated into the Canva design platform. These features enhance the design process by automating tasks, suggesting design elements, and enabling users to create stunning visuals more efficiently. Canva AI aims to empower users of all skill levels to create professional-looking designs.

Key Features

  • AI-powered design suggestions and recommendations
  • Text-to-image generation
  • Magic Eraser for removing unwanted objects from images
  • Background Remover
  • AI-driven presentation and video creation features
  • Smartmockups for showcasing designs

AI Technology

Canva AI utilizes various AI models, including deep learning and computer vision, to analyze images, understand text prompts, and generate relevant design elements. The AI algorithms are trained on a massive dataset of images and design patterns, allowing them to provide intelligent and context-aware suggestions.

Pricing
