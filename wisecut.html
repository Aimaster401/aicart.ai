What is Wisecut?

Wisecut is an AI-powered video editor designed to make editing fast and effortless. It automatically removes awkward silences, adds jump cuts, smart background music, and generates subtitles, making your videos concise and engaging.

Key Features

  • Automatic jump cuts and silence removal
  • AI-generated background music and volume ducking
  • Auto subtitles and translations
  • Audio and video pacing optimization
  • Face detection and smart zoom

AI Technology

Wisecut uses AI and machine learning to analyze speech, detect silences, and edit videos for flow and engagement automatically.
