What is Voicery?

Voicery is a high-quality AI text-to-speech (TTS) platform that creates natural-sounding synthetic voices from text. It’s designed for voice applications, virtual assistants, IVR systems, audiobooks, and content creation, offering customizable and realistic voices in multiple languages.

Key Features

  • Natural, human-like AI voices
  • Customizable voice styles and emotions
  • Multiple languages and accents
  • API for easy integration
  • Fast, cloud-based synthesis

AI Technology

Voicery uses proprietary deep learning models to deliver expressive, lifelike speech synthesis with customizable inflection and pacing.

Pricing

Custom pricing based on usage and integration needs. Free demos available on the website. Contact Voicery for a quote.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Very natural and expressive voices
  • Supports many languages and styles
  • Easy API integration for developers

Cons:

Use Cases

Perfect for virtual assistants, IVR, audiobooks, accessibility, and any application requiring lifelike synthetic speech.
