What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an advanced AI chatbot developed by OpenAI, capable of understanding natural language and generating human-like responses in real-time.

Key Features

  • Natural conversation in multiple languages
  • Answering questions, writing help, coding assistance
  • Used in education, business, and entertainment

Use Cases
