What is FlexClip?

FlexClip is an AI-powered online video maker and editor that lets users easily create, edit, and share videos. With AI tools for script to video, auto subtitles, text-to-speech, and a huge template library, FlexClip is great for marketing, education, business, and social content.

Key Features

  • AI script-to-video generator
  • Automatic subtitles and text-to-speech
  • Rich template library for all purposes
  • Stock footage, music, and photo integration
  • Easy trimming, merging, and editing tools
  • Branding and watermarking options

AI Technology

FlexClip uses AI for script analysis, automatic scene selection, subtitle generation, and voiceovers, making video creation fast and accessible.

Pricing

Free plan with watermark and export limits. Paid plans unlock high-res exports, premium assets, and advanced features. See FlexClip’s website for current pricing.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Very user-friendly and web-based
  • AI-powered automation saves time
  • Large template and stock content library

Cons:

  • Free plan has watermarks and export limits
  • Advanced features require a subscription

Use Cases

Ideal for marketers, educators, social media managers, and anyone wanting to make professional videos quickly.
