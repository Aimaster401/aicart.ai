What is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI video generator that allows you to create professional-looking videos from text using customizable avatars. It’s perfect for eLearning, training, marketing, and communication without the need for cameras or studios.

Key Features

  • AI avatars and presenters
  • Text-to-video creation
  • Multiple languages and accents supported
  • Custom avatar creation
  • Easy video editing tools
  • Templates for various use cases

AI Technology

Synthesia uses advanced generative AI and deep learning to animate avatars and sync them to your provided text, creating realistic video presentations.
