What is Krispy.ai?

Krispy.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant that helps you transcribe, summarize, and manage your online meetings effortlessly. With Krispy.ai, you get accurate meeting notes, action items, and highlights delivered automatically, saving time and improving productivity.

Key Features

  • Automatic meeting transcription
  • AI-generated summaries and highlights
  • Action items extraction
  • Supports major platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, etc.)
  • Shareable meeting notes
  • Search and organize past meetings

AI Technology

Krispy.ai uses advanced speech recognition and natural language processing to transcribe conversations and summarize key points, ensuring you never miss important details from any meeting.
