What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps you communicate clearly and effectively. It goes beyond basic grammar and spelling checks, offering suggestions for improving your writing style, tone, and clarity. Grammarly is available as a browser extension, desktop app, and mobile app, integrating seamlessly into your writing workflow.

Key Features

  • Grammar and spelling checker
  • Style and tone suggestions
  • Punctuation and conciseness suggestions
  • Vocabulary enhancement
  • Plagiarism detection
  • Personalized writing insights
  • Integration with various platforms and apps

AI Technology

Grammarly utilizes advanced natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to analyze your writing and provide personalized feedback. It identifies potential errors and suggests improvements based on context, audience, and writing goals. Grammarly's AI is constantly learning and improving to provide more accurate and relevant suggestions.

Pricing

Grammarly offers a free plan with basic grammar and spelling checks.
